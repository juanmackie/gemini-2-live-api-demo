Instructions for Connecting:
  • Press Connect: First, locate and press the "Connect" button in the interface.
  • Enable Microphone: After connecting, turn on your microphone to enable voice input and ensure to click the mic button in the bottom left.
Usage:

Alex can help you:

  • - Find out what status your tasks are in currently.Just say "Please check the status of task for me".
  • - Advise of details of a task such as authorisation details, scope of works
  • - Check the status of a defect quote. Just say "Could you check a defect quote for me".
